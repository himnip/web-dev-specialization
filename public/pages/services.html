Our Services

Great hair begins with a fresh wash by great hands here at our salon

Keratin Treatment

A keratin treatment is a process that smooths and often straightens hair. You might also hear it called a Brazilian keratin treatment or a Brazilian blowout. It involves applying products to the hair, blow-drying it, and sealing it with a flat iron.

Hair Cuts

We have professional hair dressers with years of experience to carefully cut and care for your hair.

Balayage

Balayage is a French word that means “to sweep.” In this hair color technique, highlights are hand-painted or “swept” on the surface of random sections of hair. Dye or lightener is usually painted on, starting midshaft and becoming denser as it moves down the section of hair to the ends.
